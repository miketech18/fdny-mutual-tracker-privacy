Privacy Policy

FDNY Mutual Tracker  ·  Effective date: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FDNY Mutual Tracker ("the app") handles your information. The short version: the app collects no personal data and stores everything locally on your device.

Information We Collect

We collect no information. The app does not require an account, does not connect to any server, and does not transmit any data over the internet.

Data Storage

All data you enter — including tour dates, mutual exchange records, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Android's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. No third-party SDKs that collect data are included.

Permissions

The app does not request any sensitive device permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, etc.).

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for adult FDNY firefighters and is not directed at children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
