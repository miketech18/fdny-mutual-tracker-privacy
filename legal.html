Privacy Policy

FDNY Mutual Tracker
Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FDNY Mutual Tracker ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. The App keeps ordinary schedule data on your device, while the optional My Crew features use Firebase to connect you with people you choose to add or contact.

Information We Collect

Schedule and log data — including group numbers, tour logs, RSOT dates, overtime logs, and mutual history — is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. If you enable My Crew, we collect and store the information you choose to provide for your crew profile, such as your name, company, group or FDNY reference number, email address, and optional phone number. We also receive crew requests, crew membership, mutual-board posts, and mutual-transfer records needed to operate those features.

Subscription & Billing

The App offers an optional subscription ($9.99/year) processed through the App Store and Google Play. Billing is handled entirely by Apple/Google and RevenueCat. We do not collect or store your payment information. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data to validate your subscription status. Please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy, Apple's Privacy Policy, and Google's Privacy Policy for details.

How We Use and Share Data

We use My Crew information to search for and connect users, maintain crew lists, show crew-only mutual posts, send or receive mutual requests, and import accepted mutual transfers. Information you choose to include in a crew profile or mutual post may be visible to the connected crew members or other users involved in that request. Your phone number is shared only with the firefighter involved when a Mutual Board trade is accepted.

We use Firebase/Google Cloud as a service provider to authenticate accounts and store and synchronize My Crew data. We do not sell personal information, and we do not use it for advertising. The App does not include analytics or advertising SDKs.

Data Storage, Retention, and Controls

Local schedule data is removed when you delete the App or clear its data. My Crew data is stored in Firebase while your account or the relevant crew relationship remains active. You can change or remove your crew profile, leave or remove crew connections, and request deletion of account-held data by contacting us. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no system is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for adult FDNY personnel. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
