Delete Your FDNY Mutual Tracker Account

FDNY Mutual Tracker
Last updated: July 14, 2026

If you want to delete your FDNY Mutual Tracker account and associated My Crew data, email us at fdnyscheduler@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.

What to include

Please include the following in your message so we can process the request quickly:

  • Your name
  • The email address tied to your account
  • A clear request to delete your account and associated data

What will be deleted

When we process your request, we delete your account profile and associated My Crew data, including crew membership information, crew requests, and mutual-transfer records stored for that account.

What may be retained

We may retain limited records if required for legal, tax, fraud-prevention, or security purposes, or if the data is needed to resolve disputes or enforce our terms. Any retained data is kept only as long as necessary for those purposes.

Timing

We aim to process deletion requests within a reasonable time after verifying the account ownership.

No separate data-only deletion flow

If you want to delete only some My Crew data without deleting the account, contact us and describe what you want removed. We’ll review the request and let you know what we can delete while keeping the app working.
